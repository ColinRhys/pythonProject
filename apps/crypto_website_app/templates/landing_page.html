So how do you safely buy a crypto currency like the ones listed above?

Buying crypto currencies is easy and made even easier by following the detailed steps laid out below.

Our preferred exchange is Binance. Binance not only is one of the only exchanges that has not been hacked but offers trading for a wide variety of crypto currencies. There are three main steps to buying and selling crypto currencies on Binance.

    1. Buy Bitcoin or Etherum on Cex.io. Binance does not allow you to directly deposit cash into your account, instead you need to deposit a crypto currency and that is why you need CEX.io. If the crypto currency you wish to buy on Biannce has a pair like ETH/(coin you want) buy ETH not Bitcoin to reduce the fees and transaction times.
    2. Transfer those bitcoins you just bought to your Binance account.
    3. Buy your crypto currency of choice on Binance.
    * If the crypto Currency you want is purchaseable on there is no need to transfer those coins to Biance after purchasing them on Cex.io. You will only incur more fees by transfering them to you Binance account.
